Margit Island Snake Game

Offline, self-contained HTML5 Canvas game. Two snakes (Jay & Dani), mushrooms, and Rita hazard. Audio starts after your first click/keydown (browser policy).

This ONLY changes snake speed. AI is always competitive for mushrooms.
Rita is always fast. She drops multiple poops per pass.

Rules

  • Grid: 20px cells. Snakes move 1 cell per tick.
  • Mushroom: +1 score, +1 growth for that snake.
  • Winner rule: the snake that crashes loses (even if it had more points).
  • Rita appears every 15 seconds: white 2×2 hazard moving continuously.
  • Rita drops multiple 💩 poops per pass (fart sounds).
  • Poop effect: hitting a poop shrinks your snake by 1 segment.
  • Collision with wall, self, other snake, or Rita = game over.
  • High scores saved in localStorage (per snake).
Tip: Click the game once or press any key to enable audio.
