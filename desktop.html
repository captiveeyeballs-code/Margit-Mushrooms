🐍 Margit Mushroom Adventures!

Jay and Dani are up to mischief on the Margit Island, looking for mushrooms to eat! Rita isn't happy about it and — like she always does — is talking shit about them to everyone, while trying to put a stop to their adventures. Eat as many mushrooms as you can, but don't get too high or you will slow down and Rita will catch you! Last player standing wins!

Changes snake speed only. AI is always competitive.
Rita is always fast and drops multiple 💩 per pass.

How to Play

Jay ⬆ ⬇ ⬅ ➡ Arrow keys
Dani W A S D keys
  • 🍄 Eat mushrooms to score & grow
  • The longer you get, the slower you move
  • Crash into a wall, yourself, or the other snake = out
  • 💩 Hit Rita's poop = lose a segment
  • 😱 Hit Rita = game over for that snake
  • Last player standing wins!
Tap/click the game or press Space/Enter to start.
